CryptoTrace – Privacy Policy
Last updated: 12/02/2025
CryptoTrace (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is a personal crypto and NFT market tracker that displays public market data and allows users to save a local portfolio directly on their device.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect
CryptoTrace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
The App does not use:
Login or authentication
Cloud storage
User accounts
Tracking or analytics
Advertising identifiers
Cookies

1.1 Local Portfolio Data
The App allows you to enter your crypto holdings for portfolio tracking.
This data is:
Stored locally on your device only
Never uploaded to any server
Not accessible to us or third parties
Deleted if you uninstall the App

1.2 Public Market Data
The App retrieves publicly available market data, including:
Cryptocurrency prices
NFT metadata
News headlines
via:
CoinMarketCap API
Moralis API
NewsData API
We do not send them any personal information because we do not collect any.

2. How We Use Data
We only use locally stored data to:
Display your portfolio values
Organize your watchlist
Customize the user interface
This information never leaves your device.
APIs provide public data only; we do not modify or enrich it.

3. Third-Party Services
The App communicates with third-party APIs to fetch public crypto, NFT, and news data:
CoinMarketCap
Moralis
NewsData.io
These services may log standard server information (e.g., IP address) as part of normal API requests.
CryptoTrace does not control their servers or policies.
Their policies:
https://coinmarketcap.com/privacy/
https://moralis.io/privacy-policy/
https://newsdata.io/privacy-policy/

4. No Financial Advice
All content is for informational purposes only.
CryptoTrace does not provide investment advice.

5. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data.

6. Security
Because all portfolio data remains on your device, you are responsible for device-level security (e.g., passcode, Face ID).

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Updates will appear on your publicly accessible policy URL.
